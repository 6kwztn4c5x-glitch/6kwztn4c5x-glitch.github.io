Three ways a day could feel

Same seven demo tasks in each. Ignore the polish level — feel the flow. Which one would you actually open?
01
The Path
Your day is a trail. Tap a stop to arrive; the lantern lights, the line fills, and your marker walks forward. Finish the day, reach the harbor.
02
Tides
One thing at a time, huge and calm. "Done" sends a wave through; the next thing rises. The sky behind you moves from dawn to dusk as the day completes.
03
Skylight
Every finished thing lights a star and joins the constellation. Fill the sky and your day gets a name. Days collect into a gallery.
Each one would run on the same plumbing: Todoist as the invisible state (nothing can vanish), voice capture from walks, live on every open. This choice is purely about which world your day should live in.
